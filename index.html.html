This version intentionally treats every NPC the same way. It parses visible OSRS Wiki drop tables in your browser and approximates one kill as guaranteed drops + one weighted normal drop + independent special checks. Boss-specific mechanics like Zulrah's exact double roll, rare-drop-table nesting, slayer task modifiers, diary modifiers, combat achievement modifiers and special conditional drops may not be perfectly simulated.
